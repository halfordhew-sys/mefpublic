About the Festival

What is PACE's Manningtree Earth Festival 2026?

PACE's Manningtree Earth Festival is a community-driven celebration of environmental action and awareness. Over five weeks, from May 23rd to June 30th, 2026, local volunteers and community members come together to deliver an inspiring programme of events.

Our Mission

PACE's Manningtree Earth Festival 2026 aims to provide information, knowledge, and choices so that we can all become:

✓ More aware of climate change and biodiversity loss
✓ Better equipped with practical skills and knowledge
✓ More resilient to environmental challenges
✓ Connected to our community and shared future

About PACE Manningtree

PACE (Positive Action for Climate and Environment) Manningtree is a volunteer-led organisation dedicated to environmental action in our local community. We coordinate PACE's Manningtree Earth Festival, but the events are delivered by passionate community members like you!

Learn more at: www.pacemanningtree.org.uk
